About Clean Master Privacy

Clean Master Privacy was created by anonymous developers under the codenames gamestime102 and gamestime102tv. The goal is to enhance digital privacy and security for all users. The project was revived in April 2021 and continues to evolve today.

Top Privacy Tips

  1. Use a VPN: Encrypt your internet connection to protect your data from prying eyes.
  2. Enable Two-Factor Authentication (2FA): Add an extra layer of security to your online accounts.
  3. Be Cautious with Public Wi-Fi: Avoid logging into sensitive accounts while using unsecured networks.
  4. Use Encrypted Messaging Apps: Apps like Signal and Telegram prioritize your privacy.
  5. Regularly Update Software: Stay protected by keeping your apps and operating system updated.
