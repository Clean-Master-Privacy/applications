Clean Master Privacy

About Clean Master Privacy

Clean Master Privacy was created by two anonymous people. Their code names are gamestime102 and gamestime102tv. The project focuses on privacy and security.

The Kapıkulu organization, responsible for Clean Master Privacy, was disbanded but reactivated on April 21, 2021. Clean Master Privacy, a sub-unit of Kapıkulu, is responsible for internet and device security.

Project Developments

The Clean Master Privacy project was halted for a while due to financial reasons. However, the project was revived, and the team is working on replacing lost codes with new ones.

Contribute

We have recently rebuilt the app and are focusing on our feature roadmap. We are not accepting new issues or PRs at the moment, but feel free to fork the repository for your own experiments.

Income and Donations

We will donate some of the proceeds to charities and companies to support valuable causes.

Independence

We declared our independence on April 21, 2021, and are taking new steps to become fully independent.
