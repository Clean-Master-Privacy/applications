Clean Master Privacy

About Clean Master Privacy

The Clean Master Privacy product was created by two anonymous individuals who founded gamestime102; their codenames are gamestime102 and gamestime102tv. The gamestime102 team prepared a "Kapıkulu" for Clean Master Privacy to provide privacy and security. Although this group was disbanded over time, it was reactivated on April 21, 2021. Clean Master Privacy, a sub-unit of Kapıkulu, acts as a protector, responsible for internet and device security.

Project Developments

The Clean Master Privacy project was temporarily suspended due to financial constraints. Eventually, gamestime102 and gamestime102tv revived the project. There was significant data loss during the suspension, but the gamestime102 team is actively working to replace the lost code with new versions.

Contribute

We have recently rebuilt the app and are focusing on our current feature and fix roadmap. Therefore, we are not currently accepting new issues or PRs; feel free to fork the repository for your own experiments. Thank you for your understanding and support.

Income and Donations

We will donate some of the proceeds to charities and other organizations.

Independence

We achieved our independence on April 21, 2021, and are now taking new steps toward full autonomy.
