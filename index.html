Clean Master Privacy

About Clean Master Privacy

Clean Master Privacy was first created by two anonymous people who founded gamestime102; their code names are gamestime102 and gamestime102tv. The project focuses on privacy and security.

The Kapıkulu organization, responsible for Clean Master Privacy, was disbanded but reactivated on April 21, 2021. Clean Master Privacy, a sub-unit of Kapıkulu, is responsible for internet and device security.

Project Developments

The Clean Master Privacy project was temporarily halted due to financial resources. However, gamestime102 and gamestime102tv revived the project. Despite significant data loss, the team is working to recover the lost codes with new ones.

Contribute

We have recently rebuilt the app and focused on our current feature and fix roadmap. We are not accepting new issues or PRs at the moment, but feel free to fork the repository for your own experiments. Thank you for your understanding and support!

Income and Donations

We will donate some of the proceeds to charities and companies to support valuable causes.

Independence

We declared our independence on April 21, 2021, and we are taking steps to become fully independent.
