Essential Privacy Tips

  • Use strong, unique passwords for each of your accounts.
  • Enable two-factor authentication wherever possible.
  • Be cautious with links and attachments in emails.
  • Regularly update your software to fix vulnerabilities.
  • Use a VPN to enhance your online privacy.
  • Review and adjust app permissions regularly.
